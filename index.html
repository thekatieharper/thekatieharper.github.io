Katie Harper

I've spent the last decade scaling venture-backed startups, bringing order to early-stage chaos. Now, I apply those same skills at defense contractor, SBCC, as VP of Product & Partnerships. I focus on two critical gaps: capability and capital. I connect our companies to best-in-class vendors to accelerate their execution, and I am building a platform that aligns private market investment with government funding priorities — creating a clearer path for technology to reach the warfighter.

Previously, I served as the COO of Kard, where I helped grow the company from $0 to $10M annual revenue by standing up partnerships, scaling internal infrastructure, and building out teams (finance, HR, marketing, product, engineering). Before that, I was Head of Operations at Upwave, leading a small team and helping the company scale from seed through Series B.

I studied computer science and Russian at Duke, where I now stay involved as a mentor in the entrepreneurship community. After living in both New York and San Francisco, I currently call Florida home.
