Katie Harper

I’ve spent the last decade scaling venture-backed startups in operations roles, bringing order to early-stage chaos. I now apply those same skills to the defense ecosystem as VP of Product & Partnerships at SBCC, growing the company’s network and supporting national security efforts. Previously, I served as the COO of Kard, where I helped grow the company from $0 to ~$10M annual revenue by standing up partnerships, scaling internal infrastructure, and building out teams. Before that, I was Head of Operations at Upwave, leading a small team and helping the company scale from seed through Series B.

I studied computer science and Russian at Duke, where I now stay involved as a mentor in the entrepreneurship community. After living in both New York and San Francisco, I currently call Florida home.
